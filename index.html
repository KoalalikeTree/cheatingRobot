Welcome to Three-Card Monte v2.1!

 The objective of the game is to correctly identify where the joker card is located.
To play, the player will see three cards faced up, one of which is the joker card. Rusty the robot will proceed to flip the three cards over on the table and rearrange them.
The player will then be prompted to select one of the three cards. If the player correctly identifies the joker, he wins that round.

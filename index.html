Welcome to three-card monte!

 The objective of the game is to correctly identify where the joker card is located.
To play, the player will see three cards faced up, one of which is a joker card. Rusty the robot, will then place the three cards face down on the table and will rearrange them.
The player is then prompted to select one of the three cards. If the player correctly identifies the joker card, they will win that round. There will be a total of 20 rounds, with 10 seconds pause between each round.
